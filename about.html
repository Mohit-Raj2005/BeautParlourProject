Our Journey

The Story of Fair & Glow

Celebrating over 15 years of beauty, confidence, and community in Purnia.

"Beauty is about being comfortable in your own skin."

Fair & Glow began as a small ladies' parlour on March 01, 2005. Founded by **Mrs. Kumkum Gupta** in her own home with just one staff member, it started as a hobby fueled by a passion for skincare.

What started with a single chair blossomed into a thriving local business. Today, we have a dedicated team of experts serving over 100 customers daily, equipped with the latest technology in hair and skin artistry.
